What is the Application Process of TCS BPS?

Interested Candidates should apply for TCS BPS during the Hiring Process, you must register on the links:

Step 1: Go to the TCS Next Step Portal website. Enter your username and password to sign in. This is where you start your TCS application process.

Step 2. Verification/Personal Details

It’s important to update your Aadhar Number, Name as per your Aadhar Card, and Date of Birth as per your Aadhar Card. Make sure these details are correct and error-free before submitting. Incorrect information could disqualify your application.

Step 3. Registration – Apply for the TCS BPS hiring process

Scenario A: If you already have an account, log in and fill out the application form. After completing it, click on ‘Apply for Drive’.

Scenario B: If you’re new, click ‘Register Now’, choose ‘BPS’ as the category, and fill in your details. Once completed, submit the form and click the ‘Apply for Drive’.

Important: Ensure all details in the TCS Application Form are correct and complete, including academic information and any internships or work experience.

Step 4: Choose ‘In-Centre’ for the test mode, select your preferred test center and click ‘Apply’. Remember, once you’ve chosen a test center, you can’t change it later.

Step 5: After you’ve applied, go to ‘Track Your Application’ to confirm your status. You should see ‘Applied for Drive’ as your status. This tells you that your application has been submitted successfully.

If you don’t see ‘Applied for Drive’ as your status, this indicates your application might not have gone through. You might need to check if you missed any steps or if there were errors in your application.

If everything is correct and you still don’t see the status, you might need to wait more. Sometimes it takes time for the status to update. Once your status shows ‘Applied for Drive’. This means your application is in the system and you’re registered for the drive.

Now, you have to wait for further communication from TCS. They will let you know about any next steps or instructions. Candidates must track their application status to ensure everything is progressing smoothly. If you have any concerns or questions, contact TCS support for assistance.

That’s it! You’ve completed the application process for the TCS drive. Now, all that’s left is to prepare for the test and any interviews that may follow.

TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria

Candidates who are preparing for the TCS BPS 2024 can refer to the eligibility criteria that are provided below:

Graduates from B.Com, BA, BAF, BBI, BBA, BBM, BMS, or BSc (Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology) who pass out in 2024 are eligible to apply. However, candidates should not have any pending backlogs during the TCS BPS selection process, and their courses must be completed within the stipulated duration.

It is mandatory to declare any gaps in education in the TCS application form, and the total gap should not exceed 24 months from the highest qualification. Relevant document proof will be required for education gaps.

TCS BPS Interview Process 2024

The selection process for TCS BPS hiring consists of two main rounds:

  1. Online Written Test: This is an online exam that graduates can attempt. It will test your skills and knowledge through sections such, as aptitude, reasoning, English, etc. The total duration is 65 minutes with 80 questions. Negative marking applies for incorrect answers.
  2. Interview Round: If you clear the online written test, you will be called for an interview round. In this round, TCS recruiters will assess your communication skills, personality traits, job fitment, and overall suitability for face-to-face interaction.

However, performing well in the online test to get interviewed. Then, you must clear the interview successfully to get the final job offer from TCS BPS. Stay prepared for this two-stage process.

Q1. What is BPS’s role in TCS?

Q2.What is the salary of BPS jobs in TCS?

Ans. The typical salary for Business Process Services (BPS) employees at TCS in Bangalore ranges from ₹1.8 Lakhs to ₹4.5 Lakhs per year. On average, employees with 1 to 4 years of experience earn around ₹2.7 Lakhs annually, based on data from 12 recent salaries.

Q3.What is TCS BPS eligibility?

Ans. Graduates with degrees in B. Com, BA, BAF, BBI, BBA, BBM, BMS, and BSc (in various disciplines) who will pass out in 2024, to apply for full-time positions at TCS. Candidates must not have any pending backlogs when participating in the TCS selection process.

Ans. Wear long-sleeved shirts with formal pants or skirts in solid colors, or opt for knee-length kurtas or sarees. Complete the look with formal shoes like flats, heels, or dress sandals.

Ans. Candidates have less than 2 minutes for each TCS BPS Quantitative Aptitude Section, and it’s known to be moderate to highly challenging. The topics covered include HCF & LCM, Number Systems, and Geometry.

Q6.What is TCS BPS’s full form?

Ans. TCS BPS offers non-IT and business process services, covering various industry-specific tasks like finance and accounting. Their services include combining core processes with analytics and insights to enhance enterprise operations.

Q7.Does TCS BPS provide work from home?

Ans. Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) will no longer allow employees to work from home and, must come to the office for all five working days. This means everyone has to be in the office from Monday to Friday.

Q8.Which is better BPO or BPS?

Ans. While BPO service delivery typically handled simpler tasks, BPS now covers nearly all aspects of your business when necessary. It can extend to various areas to meet the diverse needs of your business operations.
