TCS NQT Companies List – FAQs

Q1.Where can I use the TCS NQT score?

Ans. If you have taken the TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) and received a scorecard, you can use it to apply for different job roles at TCS. This includes roles like Ninja and Digital.

Q2.What is the fresher salary in TCS?

Ans. The average starting salary for freshers at TCS in India is around ₹3.6 lakhs per year for those with less than 1 to 2 years of experience. The fresher salary range at TCS India is between ₹1.8 lakhs to ₹7.0 lakhs annually.

Q3.Does TCS hire from NQT?

Ans. TCS Digital is a way for TCS to hire new graduates from engineering colleges all over India. Students who do well in this NQT test and get selected under the TCS Digital program are offered a good salary package of around 7 lakh rupees per year.

Q4.Where can I use the TCS NQT score?

Ans. If you have taken the TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) – IT Career Readiness Test and got a scorecard, the scorecard is used to apply for different job openings at TCS. This includes jobs like the Ninja role and the Digital role.

Q5.What is the highest package for TCS NQT?

Ans.TCS is conducting its National Qualifier Test (NQT) in 2024 for all graduate students. If selected through this test, you can get a salary of up to Rs. 19 lakhs per year at TCS. The last date to apply for giving this exam is 5th April 2024. Fill out the online application before this date if you appear for the TCS NQT 2024.

Q6.What happens after clearing TCS NQT?

Ans. After clearing the TCS NQT, if your score meets a company’s requirement, you’ll get an email with a link to apply for that job role. If interested, you must fill in the asked details through that application link.

Q7.Is the TCS NQT exam tough?

Ans. The TCS NQT exam has questions that are moderately to highly difficult. The quantitative aptitude (math ability) section is the toughest part of the exam. Candidates need to practice questions to do well in this math section.

Q8.How do I start preparing for TCS?

Ans. To start with TCS NQT, understand its basics and check if you’re eligible. Then, register online, fill out the application form, and prepare for the exam by reviewing the syllabus, understanding the exam pattern, and practicing mock tests to improve your chances of success.

Q9.Who is eligible for the TCS exam?

Ans. Candidates who have completed or are pursuing B.E, B.Tech, M.E, M.Tech, M.Sc, or MCA and are scheduled to graduate in 2023 are eligible to apply. Additionally, undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma students in their pre-final and final years – apply for the exam.

Q10.What is the first post in TCS?

Ans. In TCS, new employees start as ASE-T (Assistant Systems Engineer – Trainee) for a year, like a trial period. After that, they get promoted to ASE (Assistant Systems Engineer) once they complete their training.
