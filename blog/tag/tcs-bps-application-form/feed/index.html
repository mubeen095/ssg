What is the Application fee for TCS BPS?

TCS never requires candidates to pay any money during the recruitment process or for job offers. If you’re asked to pay for anything, it’s not legitimate. TCS believes in fair and transparent recruitment practices, and we don’t charge candidates for anything.

Be cautious of any requests for payment and report them to TCS immediately. Remember, legitimate job offers from TCS won’t involve any payments.

What is the Application Process of TCS BPS?

Interested Candidates should apply for TCS BPS during the Hiring Process, you must register on the links:

Step 1: Go to the TCS Next Step Portal website. Enter your username and password to sign in. This is where you start your TCS application process.

Step 2. Verification/Personal Details

It’s important to update your Aadhar Number, Name as per your Aadhar Card, and Date of Birth as per your Aadhar Card. Make sure these details are correct and error-free before submitting. Incorrect information could disqualify your application.

Step 3. Registration – Apply for the TCS BPS hiring process

Scenario A: If you already have an account, log in and fill out the application form. After completing it, click on ‘Apply for Drive’.

Scenario B: If you’re new, click ‘Register Now’, choose ‘BPS’ as the category, and fill in your details. Once completed, submit the form and click the ‘Apply for Drive’.

Important: Ensure all details in the TCS Application Form are correct and complete, including academic information and any internships or work experience.

Step 4: Choose ‘In-Centre’ for the test mode, select your preferred test center and click ‘Apply’. Remember, once you’ve chosen a test center, you can’t change it later.

Step 5: After you’ve applied, go to ‘Track Your Application’ to confirm your status. You should see ‘Applied for Drive’ as your status. This tells you that your application has been submitted successfully.

If you don’t see ‘Applied for Drive’ as your status, this indicates your application might not have gone through. You might need to check if you missed any steps or if there were errors in your application.

If everything is correct and you still don’t see the status, you might need to wait more. Sometimes it takes time for the status to update. Once your status shows ‘Applied for Drive’. This means your application is in the system and you’re registered for the drive.

Now, you have to wait for further communication from TCS. They will let you know about any next steps or instructions. Candidates must track their application status to ensure everything is progressing smoothly. If you have any concerns or questions, contact TCS support for assistance.

That’s it! You’ve completed the application process for the TCS drive. Now, all that’s left is to prepare for the test and any interviews that may follow.

TCS BPS Apply – FAQs

Q1.Who can apply for TCS BPS?

Ans. Graduates from B. Com, BA, BAF, BBI, BBA, BBM, BMS, BSc (Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology) graduating in 2024 can apply. They must have completed their course without backlogs and within the specified timeframe.

Q2.Is TCS BPS a call center?

Ans.BPS is a growing sector including various processes beyond just customer service calls. At TCS BPS, you can develop your skills according to your interests, helping you grow professionally.

Q3.How can I join TCS as a fresher?

Ans.TCS usually picks freshers through an online Exam, math, logic, and language skills are the subjects. Those who pass are then interviewed to test their computer science knowledge and programming skills.

Q4.Is the TCS BPS exam easy?

Ans. In the TCS BPS section, 5 questions will be answered within 65 minutes (shared). On average, you have less than 2 minutes per question, but the difficulty is moderate to high.

Q5.Can I join TCS after the 12th?

Ans. To join TCS, candidates must have at least a bachelor’s degree in engineering or a related field and no active backlogs. This means they shouldn’t have incomplete subjects or courses before working at TCS.

Q6.Who is the CEO of TCS?

Ans.K. Krithivasan is the CEO and MD of Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), a top IT solutions and consulting company. He leads the company in providing global IT services and solutions.

Q7.What is BPS’s role?

Ans. Business process services (BPS) handle tasks like designing and managing enterprise workflows. This includes services such as process automation and outsourcing.

Q8.Will TCS hire in 2024?

Ans.TCS is introducing its annual National Qualifier Test (NQT) for students graduating in 2024. Engineers and computer science graduates are invited to apply for Ninja and Digital fresher hiring through TCS’ aptitude and coding tests.
