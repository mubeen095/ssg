TCS NQT Admit Card 2024- FAQs

Q1.How do you download the TCS NQT admit card?

Ans. To get your admit card for the TCS NQT exam, check your email for the link. Then, log in with your TCS NQT registration number and password to see and download your admit card.

Q2.How can I check my TCS NQT admit card?

Ans.Step 1: Enter your 12-digit roll number, which was sent to your phone and email, into the “Roll Number” field. Step 2: Use your date of birth as your password.

Q3. When will I receive my TCS NQT Admit Card?

Ans.TCS Admit Cards are typically released a few days before the exam date. You will receive an email notification once the admit cards are downloaded.

Q4.What if I missed my TCS NQT exam?

Ans. Recruiters usually have a specific time for you to take the test. If you miss it, you’ll need to ask the recruiter to set another time to take the test.

Q5.Where to download the TCS NQT scorecard?

Ans. Find a link or button that says “View” or “Download” to see your TCS NQT results.

Q6.How much TCS NQT score is required for digital?

Ans. If you score above 90% on the TCS NQT written test and pass all the interview rounds, the TCS Digital job profile will be yours.

Q7.Do I need to carry a printed copy of my TCS NQT Admit Card to the exam center?

Ans. Yes, it is mandatory to carry a printed copy of your TCS Admit Card to the exam center. Candidates without a valid admit card may not be allowed to take the exam.
