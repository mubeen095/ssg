TCS NQT 2024

The TCS NQT 2024, known as the TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) is an annual examination, conducted by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to discover new talent for positions like TCS Ninja and TCS Digital. The test comprises two sections, Part A, which assesses fundamental concepts, and Part B, designed for more advanced topics essential for roles such as TCS Digital. The next TCS NQT 2024 exam will be conducted on April and May 23rd. Candidates can also check out the mock test available on our blog.

Furthermore, Part B holds particular significance for roles requiring advanced technical proficiency. Excelling in both sections is necessary for securing positions within TCS, as they test differing levels of comprehension and expertise.

What is the TCS NQT Exam 2024?

The Initial TCS NQT Exam 2024 will be conducted on May 23rd and consists of 3 tiers, TCS Ninja and TCS Digital. Check out the table below.

TCS NQT Exam 2024 consists of two categories, the Foundation Section and the Advanced Section. Let us look into the further details given below:

a. Foundation Section:

The Foundation Section of the TCS NQT exam covers the fundamental concepts and basics of computer science and programming. It tests your understanding of topics including data structures, algorithms, programming languages, and problem-solving skills. This section will evaluate your foundational knowledge, which is essential for any software development role.

b. Advanced Section:

The Advanced Section of the TCS NQT exam has more complex and advanced topics within computer science. It includes questions, system design, database management, computer networks, operating systems, and software engineering principles. This section assesses your ability to apply your knowledge to solve intricate problems and handle more intricate programming challenges.

TCS NQT Foundation Section

This Foundation Section – the TCS NQT Exam 2024 consists of Verbal, Numerical, and Reasoning Ability.

a.Verbal Ability: This part tests your skills in understanding written English. You will read short passages and answer questions based on them. It also checks how well you can communicate your thoughts clearly in writing. Good vocabulary and grammar knowledge will help you perform better in this section.

b.Numerical Ability: In this section, you will solve math problems involving numbers, calculations, and data interpretation. You should know basic arithmetic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Some questions may also require understanding charts, graphs, and tables to analyze numerical information.

c.Reasoning Ability: This section tests your logical thinking and problem-solving skills. You will be given puzzles, patterns, or situations where you should apply logic to find solutions. This section requires you to think critically, identify relationships between different elements, and draw conclusions based on the given information

What are the TCS NQT 2024 Interview Rounds?

Technical Interview

The topics that are commonly asked in the interview rounds of TCS NQT 2024 are provided below:

  1. C Programming: Solve simple problems to grasp programming concepts.
  2. Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA): This will help you with complex problem-solving.
  3. Object-Oriented Programming (OOP): Understand inheritance and polymorphism.
  4. Software Engineering: Learn about development cycles and project management.
  5. Showcase Your Final Year Project: Demonstrate your skills and experience.
  6. Master Procedural Programs: Break down problems into manageable steps.
  7. Solve Palindrome Problems: Practice identifying words or phrases that read the same forwards and backward.
  8. Solve Palindrome Problems: Practice identifying words or phrases that read the same forwards and backward.
  9. Understand Recursion: Learn how functions call themselves to solve problems.

What are the Objectives of the TCS NQT Qualifier Test?

The main objectives of the TCS NQT Qualifier Test are the following:

  1. Multi-Level Assessments – These will test the fresher’s skills and competencies. The TCS NQT 2024 exam has different levels or sections to evaluate their skills and abilities. It is designed to assess their knowledge, problem-solving abilities, and overall competencies required for entry-level roles in the company.
  2. Entry-Level Employment – This will analyze the competency in the cognitive tasks: The primary purpose of the TCS NQT exam is to assess candidates for entry-level positions within the company. It focuses, on evaluating their cognitive abilities, which refer to skills, reasoning, problem-solving, and critical thinking.
  3. Cognitive Skills – This will check the cognitive skills, necessary for entry-level jobs: Cognitive skills are mental abilities that enable individuals to process information, think critically, and solve problems effectively. The TCS NQT exam aims to test these cognitive skills, for entry-level jobs in the company.

Benefits Of the TCS NQT Exam

The TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) offers a promising career opportunity for engineering students at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). Academic scores or college reputation do not matter. Performing well in this standardized online test can lead to direct shortlisting for corporate roles at TCS through campus placements.

  • TCS has been India’s top IT recruiter since 2006 with over 592,000 consultants.
  • Merit-based success in the NQT can open doors to job interviews or advanced technical training through the TCS Ignite program.
  • Even if not selected, taking the TCS NQT is an invaluable experience for personal and professional growth.
  • The test showcases technical skills and aptitude, enhancing visibility among potential employers.
  • The best part is that the NQT is conducted nationwide with no registration fees. This gives engineers a risk-free opportunity to participate in this national-level qualifier and showcase their talent.

The TCS National Qualifier Test provides engineering students a fair chance to kickstart promising careers at TCS through a merit-based online assessment. A good performance can directly lead to job opportunities or advanced training, regardless of academic credentials. The test experience itself is valuable for growth.

TCS NQT 2024 -FAQs

Q1. What is the last date of TCS NQT registration in 2024?

Ans. Candidates pursuing B.Tech/B.E/M.Tech/M.C.A/M.Sc degrees and graduating in 2024 are eligible to apply for the TCS NQT exam, a general ability test conducted on 23rd May 2024 with the application deadline being 11th May 2024.

Q2. What is the mode of exam for TCS NQT 2024?

Ans. The TCS NQT Test Pattern 2024 is an integrated test, with 2 sections, Foundation and Advanced.

Q3.Is TCS NQT held twice a year?

Ans. The TCS NQT exam is a computer-based test, conducted twice a year.

Q4.What is the salary after cracking TCS NQT?

Ans. The salary of graduates of TCS NQT is 3.26 LPA (Ninja ) to 7 LPA (Digital).

Q5. Which year of passing (YoP) is being considered for the TCS Freshers Hiring Process through the TCS NQT?

Ans. Candidates who graduated in the 2024 batch can apply for the TCS NQT Exam.

Q6.Is TCS NQT free or paid?

Ans. No, The first NQT test, is free to attempt

Q7.Is TCS 5 days a week?

Ans. TCS Employees are supposed to work for 5 days a week.

Q8.Does TCS allow ex-employees to rejoin after 5 years?

Ans.No, TCS has a strict policy that prevents recruiters from hiring ex-employees.

Q9.How many freshers is TCS Hiring?

Ans. TCS is hiring 10,000 freshers, which will begin in the second phase and include this TCS ion for Ninja, Digital, and Prime.

Q10. Do I need to create my profile under ‘BPS’ or ‘IT’?

Ans. Yes, candidates should create a profile under IT for registration of NQT.

Q11.I have incorrectly created my profile under ‘BPS’. What should I do now?

Ans. Send an email to tcsbps.support@tcs.com to delete your profile in BPS. After your profile is deleted, create a new one under IT.

Q12. How will I know about my TCS NQT test schedule / test-related information?

Ans. You’ll receive information about the test from our test partner, TCS iON, at careers.tcshub@mail.digialm.com. They’ll email you all the details you need to know.

Q13. How will we get the results of the NQT test?

Ans. Shortlisted Candidates will get more information about the next steps from the TCS Talent Acquisition Team through the email they registered.
