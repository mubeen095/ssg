TCS BPS Hiring Process

TCS BPS (Tata Consultancy Services Business Process Services) has started its 2024 hiring season. The company is known for its comprehensive and rigorous hiring process to attract top talent. Candidates can expect to undergo online assessments, followed by multiple interviews to evaluate their skills, aptitude, and cultural fit.

TCS BPS is opened exclusively for the 2024 batch, regardless of their degree discipline – Arts, Commerce, or Science, to apply for exciting job opportunities within the company. If you are completing your graduation in 2024, you can explore various career options at TCS BPS across different fields and roles. TCS BPS is open to hiring talented individuals from diverse educational backgrounds, providing a platform to kickstart their professional journey. This blog is about the TCS BPS Hiring Process 2024.

TCS BPS offers a range of job roles across various domains, catering to fresh graduates and experienced professionals. The compensation packages are competitive and align with industry standards, with opportunities for career growth and advancement within the organization.

What is TCS BPS Hiring Process 2024?

TCS BPS (Tata Consultancy Services Business Process Services) conducts its hiring examination through TCS iON, the company’s official test provider. All examination-related information, such as test dates, timings, and instructions, will be communicated to candidates via TCS iON. It’s important to note that the interviews for TCS BPS will not be held on the same day as the TCS Smart BPS Hiring Test.

Furthermore, if you successfully clear the TCS Smart BPS Hiring Test, you will receive an email on your email ID with details about the interview schedule and further instructions. The interview process is a crucial step in the hiring process, and candidates will be evaluated based on their performance in the interview rounds. Stay alert and check your email regularly for updates from TCS BPS regarding the interview process.

Steps Involved in TCS BPS Hiring Process 2024

The TCS BPS Hiring process is listed below. Candidates can look at their hiring process for the TCS BPS here:

Online Written Test

The TCS BPS 2024 hiring process begins with an online written test. This first step is designed to evaluate your aptitude skills. The company wants to assess your general reasoning, problem-solving abilities, and basic knowledge through this initial round. Candidates can also refer to the Tata Consultancy Services(TCS) BPS Syllabus for reference.

Interview Round

After clearing the online written test, you will proceed to the interview round, a crucial part of the TCS BPS hiring procedure. During an interview, the focus will be assessing your confidence, leadership qualities, and communication skills. The interviewers will ask questions to gauge your ability to express yourself clearly, handle challenging situations, and demonstrate your potential for leadership roles. They want to understand your personality, thought process, and how you might fit into the company’s culture. Preparing well for this round is essential, as it plays a significant role in determining your candidature for the job.

What is the Salary of the TCS BPS Job role?

At TCS BPS in India, the typical monthly salary for employees is around ₹2,25,000 per month. This is the base pay that most BPS professionals can expect to earn.

However, in addition to the base salary, TCS BPS provides cash compensation or bonuses to their employees.BPS employees receive around ₹2,10,000 yearly. This additional amount can vary quite a bit, ranging from a lower limit of ₹36,000 to a higher limit of ₹3,84,000 per year.

However, the Bonus the employee will get depends on factors like their performance, role, experience, and profits. So while the base salary is fixed, the total earning potential can be significantly higher with the added cash incentives and bonuses.

TCS BPS Hiring Process- FAQs

Q1.How many rounds are there in BPS in TCS?

Ans. There are initially 2 or 3 rounds in the TCS BPS Hiring Process. The eligibility includes applicants from BA, BCOM, BBA, BAF, BBI, BBM, BMS, BSc IT/ CS/ General, BCA, and BCS backgrounds who write the exam.

Q2.What positions does TCS BPS hire for?

Ans. TCS BPS hires for various positions across various domains including customer support, finance and accounting, human resources, IT-enabled services, and more. Common roles include customer service associates, financial analysts, HR specialists, and IT support specialists.

Q3. How can I apply for a job at TCS BPS?

Ans. You can apply for a job at TCS BPS through the company’s official careers portal or job postings on various job search platforms. Make sure to review the job requirements and submit your application online.

Q4. What is the typical hiring process at TCS BPS?

Ans. The typical hiring process at TCS BPS involves several stages: candidates first submit an online application, undergo screening and an aptitude test, and then face one or more interview rounds. Those who perform well in the interviews receive a job offer from TCS BPS.

Q5.What kind of skills and qualifications are required to work at TCS BPS?

Ans. The specific skills and qualifications required depend on the role you are applying for. However, some common skills and qualifications include: Strong communication skills, Analytical and problem-solving skills, Basic computer skills, and relevant educational background (e.g., degree in finance for financial analysts).

Q6. Is there any training provided after joining TCS BPS?

Ans. Yes, TCS BPS provides comprehensive training to new hires to equip them with the necessary skills and knowledge required for their roles. The training may include domain-technical training and soft skills training.

Q7.How can I stay updated on job openings at TCS BPS?

Ans. Candidates can bookmark the Skillvertex blog, and get regular updates on the TCS BPS Hiring process and examination.

Q8.How can I contact TCS BPS for further inquiries about the hiring process?

Ans. For further inquiries about the hiring process at TCS BPS, you can contact the company’s HR department through their official website or reach out to them via email or phone provided in the job postings.

Q9.Are there opportunities for career growth at TCS BPS?

Ans. Yes, TCS BPS offers ample opportunities for career growth and development. Employees can progress within their current roles or explore opportunities to transition into different roles or departments based on their interests and skills.

Q10.What is the work environment like at TCS BPS?

Ans. TCS BPS offers a professional and collaborative work environment where employees, will work with leading global clients across various industries. The company values diversity, innovation, and continuous learning.
