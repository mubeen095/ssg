Factors Determining the Cutoff of the TCS NQT

The may Cut- off will be predicted by Tata Consultancy Services according to the following factors given below

1. Difficulty Level of the Test: After analyzing how difficult the questions in the test were, TCS adjusts the cutoff score accordingly. If the test was relatively easy, the cutoff may be higher, if it was challenging, the cutoff may be lower.

2. Number of Candidates and Their Performance: The cutoff score is influenced by the total number of candidates who took the test and how well they performed. It is mainly calculated according to the number of candidates who scored high, the cutoff may be higher to select the top performers.

3. Target Number of Hires: TCS will hire 10,000 freshers from the NQT. Based on this target and the performance of the candidates, they determine the cutoff score to select the desired number of candidates.

What next after the TCS NQT Result?

After getting the TCS NQT Result, candidates can attempt the interview with different companies according to the TCS NQT Scorecard. Carry the TCS NQT Scorecard before going for the TCS NQT Interview.

Step 1: If you score above the cutoff set by the company in their online aptitude test, you will receive an email from them. This email will contain a link that allows you to apply for the job. Companies that accept the TCS NQT Score include – TCS, Reliance, Tata Motors, Tata Electric, and Ramco.

Step 2: If you are still interested in the job role and wish to proceed further, go to TCS NQT’s official website and fill out the application form. This form will require you to enter details about yourself, such as your personal information, educational qualifications, work experience (if any), and other relevant details.

Step 3: After you have submitted the application form, the company will review your resume and the information you provided. If they find your profile suitable for the role, they will send you another email, typically as an interview call letter. This email will contain details about the interview process, such as the date, time, and interview (in-person or virtual).

It’s important to note that receiving an interview call does not guarantee a job offer. The interview is the next step in the selection process, where the company will assess your skills, knowledge, and suitability for the role through various rounds of interviews.

TCS NQT 2024

The TCS NQT 2024, known as the TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) is an annual examination, conducted by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to discover new talent for positions like TCS Ninja and TCS Digital. The test comprises two sections, Part A, which assesses fundamental concepts, and Part B, designed for more advanced topics essential for roles such as TCS Digital. a. Foundation Section:

The Foundation Section of the TCS NQT exam covers the fundamental concepts and basics of computer science and programming. It tests your understanding of topics including data structures, algorithms, programming languages, and problem-solving skills. The Advanced Section of the TCS NQT exam has more complex and advanced topics within computer science.
