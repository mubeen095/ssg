TCS NQT Syllabus 2024- FAQs

Q1.Is TCS NQT 2024 online or offline?

Ans. TCS NQT Exam is conducted online.

Q2.What is TCS NQT 2024?

Ans. TCS NQT 2024 is a test by Tata Consulting Services for freshers (Ninja, Digital, and Prime** Aspirants) to assess their skills in aptitude and specific domains. It helps TCS identify talented candidates.

Q3.Is there a sectional cutoff in TCS NQT 2024?

Ans.No, there is no sectional cut-off in the TCS NQT

Q4.What is the syllabus for the TCS NQT test?

Ans.The TCS NQT 2023 exam has sections on Quantitative Aptitude, Verbal Ability, Reasoning Ability, and Programming Logic. Know the syllabus and pattern to plan your prep effectively.

Q5.Is TCS NQT 2024 paid?

Ans. Candidates will be provided an annual stipend of 3.6 lakh to 7 lakh depending upon their job position.

Q6.How many times a year is the TCS NQT 2024 exam?

Ans. The TCS NQT exam is a computer-based test held twice a year in May and November. It lasts for 180 minutes and has 92 questions.

Q7.Can we write TCS Nqt at home?

Ans. The TCS NQT test occurs every quarter, allowing you to choose the subject you wish to be tested on, such as aptitude or domain-specific skills. You can take the test either from home or at designated exam centers.

Q8.Does TCS allow 1 backlog?

Ans. Candidates are allowed to have a backlog, they must clear it before joining TCS. TCS Aspirants can have an education gap of not more than 24 months and should submit relevant documents before attempting the exam.
