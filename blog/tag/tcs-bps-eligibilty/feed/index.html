TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) has announced TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria 2024 for Business Process Services (BPS) roles. Eligible candidates can apply through the official TCS website.

Once the application is submitted, candidates will receive an admit card to download from the website. The age requirement is between 18 and 28 years. This blog has listed the TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria.

To be considered for selection, candidates must meet the TCS BPS eligibility criteria provided on the website.

TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria 2024-In Detail

Candidates should fill out apply form according to the TCS BCS Eligibility Criteria. Interested Candidates for this BPS job role can look at the eligibility criteria below.

Age Limit

The age limit to attempt the TCS BPS Exam is 18 years. Aspirants applying for the BPS Post should not exceed 28 yrs. Further, TCS won’t qualify the candidates more than the age limit.

To know more about the Application process.

National Qualification

Candidates who have completed graduation in Science, Arts, and Commerce in 2024 are eligible to apply for the TCS BPS hiring process.

Nationality

Interested Applicant should belong to Indian Nationality. The recruitment process is only for Indian Citizens. Additionally, they should submit the national identity proof during the application process.

Number of Attempts

TCS (Tata Consultancy Services ) doesn’t mention the number of attempts for the TCS BPS process. The applicants will apply for the exam only if they fulfill the eligibility criteria of the BPS Examination.

Experience

Work Experience is not necessary for TCS BPS Eligibility. Further, candidates without any experience can fill out the application form.

TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria 2024: Important Points

The applicant should read the points on TCS BPS Eligibility Criteria.

  • The candidates should refer to the eligibility Criteria before the application form, available on the Tata Consultancy Services Portal.
  • Candidates can download the TCS BPS Admit Card from the official portal after the application form is filled out.
  • The age limit to apply for this examination is from 18 years to 28 years.
  • Candidates should satisfy the eligibility of the TCS BPS, to be eligible for the hiring process.
  • Candidates should fill in the correct information otherwise, if they provide incorrect details, their application will be considered invalid.
  • Moreover, Strict action will be filed against the candidates for malpractices and giving incorrect details.

To summarize the key eligibility criteria for the TCS BPS recruitment, candidates must be between 18 and 28 years old. They should fill out the application form on the official TCS website and download their admit card from the portal. Meeting the educational qualifications and other requirements mentioned on the website is crucial to be eligible.

TCS will announce the date for the online assessment test soon, which candidates have to appear for. Only those satisfying all the eligibility conditions laid out by TCS will progress further in the selection process for the Business Process Services role.
