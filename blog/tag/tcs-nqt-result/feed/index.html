How to check TCS NQT Scorecard 2024?

Graduates can refer to the steps below to download the TCS NQT Scorecard 2024 from the TCS ION website. Check it out and download your NQT Scorecard 2024.

  1. Go to the TCS ION Official website and click the past results.
  2. The home page will take you to the TCS Candidate login portal.
  3. Log in with your User number and password in the login portal to access your NQT Results. The Registration number can be entered as the username and your mobile number will be the password. Enter the details carefully
  4. Then, you will be directed to the application form in which you can see two sections, applicant details and scorecard.
  5. Select the Scorecard and download the scorecard of your NQT Exam.
  6. In the scorecard section, you can view the scorecards of TCS NQT – Cognitive, TCS NQT -Software Engineer, TCS NQT – Information Technology, and TCS NQT Psychometric.
  7. Choose the required TCS NQT Scorecard from this list and select Download the Scorecard, which appears in orange.
  8. Download the TCS NQT Scorecard and take a hard copy of the TCS Scorecard results pdf as it can be useful for your Interview process.

TCS NQT Cut off 2024

TCS didn’t have any cutoff for the online aptitude test, the NQT (National Qualifier Examination). The hiring process will consider the number of candidates and the colleges participating in the TCS (Tata Consultancy Services).

However, we don’t know about the cut-off – Tata Consultancy Services(TCS). The fixed score or the required percentage needed to pass the NQT(National Qualifier Test ) is unknown.

Factors Determining the Cutoff of the TCS NQT

The may Cut- off will be predicted by Tata Consultancy Services according to the following factors given below

1. Difficulty Level of the Test: After analyzing how difficult the questions in the test were, TCS adjusts the cutoff score accordingly. If the test was relatively easy, the cutoff may be higher, if it was challenging, the cutoff may be lower.

2. Number of Candidates and Their Performance: The cutoff score is influenced by the total number of candidates who took the test and how well they performed. It is mainly calculated according to the number of candidates who scored high, the cutoff may be higher to select the top performers.

3. Target Number of Hires: TCS will hire 10,000 freshers from the NQT. Based on this target and the performance of the candidates, they determine the cutoff score to select the desired number of candidates.

What next after the TCS NQT Result?

After getting the TCS NQT Result, candidates can attempt the interview with different companies according to the TCS NQT Scorecard. Carry the TCS NQT Scorecard before going for the TCS NQT Interview.

Step 1: If you score above the cutoff set by the company in their online aptitude test, you will receive an email from them. This email will contain a link that allows you to apply for the job. Companies that accept the TCS NQT Score include – TCS, Reliance, Tata Motors, Tata Electric, and Ramco.

Step 2: If you are still interested in the job role and wish to proceed further, go to TCS NQT’s official website and fill out the application form. This form will require you to enter details about yourself, such as your personal information, educational qualifications, work experience (if any), and other relevant details.

Step 3: After you have submitted the application form, the company will review your resume and the information you provided. If they find your profile suitable for the role, they will send you another email, typically as an interview call letter. This email will contain details about the interview process, such as the date, time, and interview (in-person or virtual).

It’s important to note that receiving an interview call does not guarantee a job offer. The interview is the next step in the selection process, where the company will assess your skills, knowledge, and suitability for the role through various rounds of interviews.

TCS NQT Result 2024- FAQs

Q1.What is the TCS NQT?

Ans. The TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT) is a standardized test conducted by Tata Consultancy Services to assess the competencies and skills of candidates seeking employment with TCS and other participating companies.

Q2. When will the TCS NQT 2024 results be announced?

Ans. The TCS NQT 2024 results are typically announced a few weeks after the exam. Please check the official TCS NQT website or your registered email for specific dates.

Q3. How can I check my TCS NQT 2024 result?

Ans. You can check your result by logging into your TCS iON account with your credentials. The result link will be available on the dashboard.

Q4. What details are required to check the result?

Ans. To check your TCS NQT result, you need your login credentials (email ID and password) used during registration.

Q5. What information will be available on the result page?

Ans. The result page will display your score, percentile, and qualification status. You may also see section-wise scores and feedback.

Q6. Will I receive any notification when the results are declared?

Ans. Yes, TCS will send an email notification to your registered email ID when the results are declared. You can also check the TCS NQT website for updates.

Q7. What should I do if I forget my login credentials?

Ans. You can use the ‘Forgot Password’ option on the TCS iON login page to reset it. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email ID.

Q8. How is the TCS NQT score calculated?

Ans. The TCS NQT score is calculated based on your performance in different sections of the test, including verbal ability, reasoning ability, numerical ability, and programming skills (if applicable).

Q9. What is a good score in TCS NQT 2024?

Ans. A good score varies each year based on the difficulty level and the overall performance of candidates. However, scoring above the 60th percentile is generally considered competitive.

Q10. Will the TCS NQT result affect my chances of getting a job with TCS?

Ans. Yes, the TCS NQT result is a significant factor in the recruitment process. Candidates who meet the required cut-off marks will proceed to further rounds such as interviews and technical assessments.

Q11. Can I retake the TCS NQT if I am not satisfied with my score?

Ans. Yes, TCS allows candidates to retake the NQT. You can register for the next available test date to improve your score.

Q12.What are the next steps after qualifying for the TCS NQT?

Ans. If you qualify for the TCS NQT, you will receive further instructions regarding the subsequent rounds of the selection process, which may include technical interviews, HR interviews, and other assessments.

Q13. Where can I find more information or assistance regarding the TCS NQT result?

Ans. For more information or assistance, you can visit the TCS NQT official website or contact TCS support via the helpdesk contact details provided on their website.

Q14. Are there any resources to help prepare for the TCS NQT?

Ans. Skillvertex provides the TCS NQT Mock test, which will help you improve your coding and technical skills.
